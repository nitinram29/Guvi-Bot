frequently asked questions

What is Civil CAD?

Civil CAD is a software that helps the Civil & Architectural professionals to create Detailed Floor plans, Section & Elevation drawings which is very essential for the building execution.

Is CAD important in a Civil engineering career?

Yes! CAD plays an important role in the civil engineering career as it can convey various details, including dimensions, section detailings, joinery details,etc., CAD is essential in creating civil and architectural detail drawings.

What is the salary of an CAD Civil engineer?

The salary of a Civil CAD engineer ranges from ₹3 LPA to ₹20 LPA, with an average salary of ₹5 Lakhs. 

How do I become a Civil / Architectural CAD designer?

You can enroll in CAD Civil course online. The Zen class-Autodesk certified civil CAD course will comprehensively cover all the in-demand tools that will help you accelerate your career in Architecture & Civil engineering. You’ll be guided by industry experts, & provided with guaranteed placement support to crack your dream role as a Civil or Architectural CAD designer.

How long will it take me to become a Civil / Architectural CAD engineer?

You can finish the Zen-class Civil CAD course within 6.5 months by attending the weekend batch to gain top-notch skills & develop a competitive advantage over other civil engineers.

Do Civil engineers need to know CAD?

CAD(computer-aided design) is a software that uses computer technology to produce 2-dimensional (2D) and 3-dimensional (3D) images of architectural or civil designs. Civil engineers will use the basic tools in CAD to create many detailed building drawings.

Does CAD need coding?

Essentially, you don’t need programming skills to get started with the Civil CAD course. But, it is preferred to have some programming knowledge to understand python scripting used for CAD design & analysis.

Will I get an Autodesk certificate with this CAD Civil course?

Yes, you will receive a globally recognised skill certificate accredited by Autodesk, IITM Pravartak & GUVI.

What software will I learn in this Civil CAD course?

Upon enrolling in the Zen class Civil CAD course, you will learn AutoCAD, Revit, 3ds Max, etc

Will there be Live classes?

Yes, This Civil CAD course offers live online classes during weekends for 6.5 months of course duration.
