FAQ'S

Is this Motion Graphics course conducted completely online?

Yes, This Motion Graphics course is an online self-paced learning course of Zen-class career programs. The online course modules comprehensively covers the core topics in motion graphics of animated graphic design career domain.

What is the course fee for this Motion Graphics course?

Is there currently a high demand for Motion Graphics in various industries?

Motion Graphics professionals can find work in industries like retail, marketing, tech videos, video games, television, movies, and any platform with visual media. Famous motion graphics studios worldwide include Explainify, Curveball, FEVR, and IdeaRocket.

Typically, how long does it take to complete a comprehensive course in Motion Graphics?

It would not take more than 120 hours to cover a comprehensive course in design & animation. This course is meticulously designed to cover all aspects of motion graphics in a comfortable manner for learners.

Is pursuing a career in Motion Graphics a wise decision for individuals?

Motion Graphics skills are a part of highly sought skillset across many industries. You can find yourself professionally settled in domains like films, television, digital advertising, and social media marketing. In this digital era, the online content creators are heavily using motion graphics skills to build their brands and establish their productions professionally.

What role does Motion Graphics play in the visual effects (VFX) industry?

Motion Graphics is a part of animated graphic design, while the Visual Effects combines the computer generated imagery(false ones) with existing footage to create realistic scenes. The animations of motion graphics will play an additional role in creating realistic characters and believable actions.

Is motion graphics primarily a 2D or 3D medium, or does it incorporate elements of both?

Motion graphics are digital animations & are usually 2D in nature. However, with latest software technologies like Adobe After effects, one can produce dazzling 3D effects with the medium.

What software applications are commonly used in the creation of motion graphics?

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects & Adobe Premier Pro are some the most popularly used software applications in the creation of motion graphics. These top technologies are covered in our Zen class-certified motion graphics course.

Is the field of animation considered to be a challenging career path?

The motion graphics career field can be easier path for aspirants with passion & creativity to create vivid illustrations, visuals & videos. If the skills are acquired through hands-on experience then motion graphics / animation isn’t a challenging career path.

What is the typical salary range for a 2D animator in India?

A 2D animator in India earns a national average salary of ₹25,000 per month in India. But with cuting-edge skills of motion graphics, a certified professional can earn salaried ranging from ₹7 Lakhs to ₹17Lakhs.
