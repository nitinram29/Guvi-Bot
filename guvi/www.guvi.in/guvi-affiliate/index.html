Frequently Asked Questions

For which GUVI EdTech Products will I receive advertising income?

As of now, 3 EdTech Products on GUVI are eligible for advertising income in the affiliate program. 

1. Zen class career programs

2. GUVI Tech courses (self-paced)

3. HackerKid gamified learning platform for kids

Get to know more about the ad income on each product here.

Will I be able to earn advertising income if the user is already an existing GUVI user?

Yes, if the user makes a purchase through your affiliate code/link then you will be eligible for the income.

If a user goes to one GUVI’s EdTech product through my link & purchases another GUVI’s EdTech product, will I still get Ad Income?

Yes, you will get advertising income for each eligible GUVI EdTech product that is purchased through your affiliate code/link within the attribution period.

Will I receive multiple ad incomes if a single user purchases multiple GUVI products?

Yes, if the user makes multiple purchases on the eligible GUVI products through your affiliate code/link, then you will receive the ad income.

Is there any fee or cost associated with this affiliate program?

No, there is no fee associated with GUVI’s affiliate program. You can register yourself for free to become an affiliate. Register now.

When will I receive my advertising income?

You will be eligible for advertising income if the user through your promotion/referral code makes a purchase within the order attribution period of 30 days.
