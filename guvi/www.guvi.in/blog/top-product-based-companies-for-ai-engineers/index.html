1. Google

Google is the first company in our list of companies that are a great place to work for AI engineers. Although it doesn’t need any formal introduction, Google is an American multinational technology company that specializes in Internet-related services and products founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. Google is known for its search engine, online advertising technologies, cloud computing, software, and hardware.

The company has a reputation for being one of the most innovative companies in the world and has a culture that encourages open dialogue and the free flow of ideas. Google is a leader in AI and data analytics, and is on a massive AI acquisition binge, having acquired a number of AI startups in the last several years. Consequentially, it has shown eagerness to hire AI engineers as they are deeply invested in advancing their AI initiatives.

Key Products:

  • Google Search
  • Google Maps
  • YouTube
  • Google Drive
  • Google Photos
  • Google Assistant
  • Google Translate

Average Salary: ₹6.1LPA-₹1.2 Crore PA

Ratings:  4.4/5 

Apply here: google.com/careers.

2. Amazon

Next up, we have Amazon. It is an American multinational technology company that focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. It was founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994. Amazon is known for its online shopping platform, Amazon Prime subscription service, and its cloud computing platform, Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Amazon is a phenomenal place for AI Engineers as the company has a culture that fosters innovation, customer obsession, and a bias for action. The company offers a comprehensive suite of AI services that integrate seamlessly with its robust infrastructure. It has been proactive in modernizing its AI capabilities, making it a fertile ground for AI engineers who are looking to work on cutting-edge technologies.

Key Products:

  • Amazon Prime
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Amazon Alexa
  • Amazon Fire TV
  • Amazon Kindle

Average Salary: ₹4.0-₹32.0 LPA

Ratings: 4.1/5

Apply here: Amazon.jobs.

The next company on our list is Meta (formerly Facebook). Meta is an American social media conglomerate that was founded by Mark Zuckerberg in 2004. The company is known for its social networking platform, Facebook, and its subsidiaries, including Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus VR.

Meta has a culture that fosters innovation, collaboration, and responsible AI among its stakeholders. It has been investing heavily in AI to enhance user experience and content relevance. Meta has been acquiring AI startups and has a dedicated AI research lab, making it an attractive destination for AI engineers interested in applying AI to social and virtual spaces.

Key Products:

  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • WhatsApp

4. Microsoft

Moving to the next favorite company for AI specialists, we have Microsoft. It’s an American multinational technology company that develops, licenses, and sells computer software, consumer electronics, and personal computers. It was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975. The company is known for its Windows operating system and Office productivity suite.

Microsoft has a great work culture that follows global employment standards that fosters collaboration, innovation, education, and responsible AI among its stakeholders. It has included a range of AI services through its Azure cloud platform. The company’s investment in OpenAI and its focus on machine learning and cognitive services make it a compelling choice for AI engineers looking to work on scalable and impactful projects.

Key Products:

  • Windows Operating System
  • Microsoft Office Suite
  • Microsoft Surface
  • Microsoft Teams
  • Microsoft Dynamics 365

Average Salary: ₹10-₹40 LPA

Ratings: 4.2/5

Apply here: microsoft.com/careers.

5. NVIDIA

A well-deserving candidate in the list of top companies for AI engineers is NVIDIA. NVIDIA Corporation is an American multinational technology company that specializes in computer hardware, middleware, and software and provides hosting and consulting services in areas ranging from mainframe computers to nanotechnology. NVIDIA boasts an exciting and conducive work culture that encourages innovation, creativity, and reliance.

NVIDIA is a leader in the field of artificial intelligence, with its GPUs being used extensively for deep learning applications. The company’s strategic shift towards AI technologies and its collaborations with research institutions make it a compelling choice for AI engineers interested in working on the hardware aspects of AI, as well as its applications in various industries.

Key Products:

  • Graphics processing units (GPUs)
  • Central processing units (CPUs)
  • Chipsets
  • Drivers
  • Collaborative software

Highest Salary: ₹3.2-₹35 LPA

Ratings: 4.2/5

Apply here: nvidia.com/careers

Adobe, as shown by reviews, positions itself as a great employer with a great work environment and work culture that promotes innovation and progress. It has been integrating AI to make its suite smarter and more intuitive. The company’s focus on AI-driven design and content creation tools offers a unique platform for AI engineers interested in the intersection of AI, design, and user experience.

FAQs

Which is the best company to work in as an AI engineer?

The “best” company for an AI engineer can vary based on individual preferences, but globally recognized firms like Google, Microsoft, and Amazon are often highly rated as the best companies to work as an AI engineer given their work culture, innovation, and compensation. In India, companies like Zoho, Freshworks, and Reliance Jio are also making strides in AI.

Which company pays the most to an AI engineer?

Compensation varies by location and experience, but large tech companies like NVIDIA, Microsoft, OpenAI, Google, and Amazon, are known for offering competitive salaries to AI engineers as high as $179,451 per annum. In India, multinational corporations and well-funded startups often provide higher pay packages.

Are AI engineers in high demand?

Yes, AI engineers are in high demand due to the growing importance of artificial intelligence in various industries, including healthcare, finance, and e-commerce. In fact, the job market is flourishing for AI engineers as more companies are looking to integrate AI into their products and services.

What is the role of an AI Engineer in a product-based company?

In a product-based company, an AI engineer is mostly responsible for designing, developing, and deploying AI algorithms and models that enhance the product’s capabilities. They work closely with product managers and data scientists to create AI features that provide value to the end-users.

How to apply for AI engineering positions in top product-based companies?

To apply for AI engineering roles in top product-based companies, you can visit their careers page online or leverage professional networking platforms like LinkedIn. In India, job portals like Naukri and Shine also frequently list such opportunities.
