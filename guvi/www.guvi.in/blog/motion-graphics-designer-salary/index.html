Are you planning to become a motion graphics designer or animator? Do you know how much a motion graphics designer gets paid in India and other foreign countries? As per AmbitionBox, the average Motion Graphics designer salary in India is Rs 4 lakhs per annum. The question of prospective salary is natural for anyone trying to build a career in this field.

Motion graphics have become extremely popular with the rise in demand for better aesthetics and more engaging graphics when it comes to marketing. That’s why most of the big marketing giants and brands are actively hiring motion graphic professionals to ensure an attractive visual appeal for their products and services.

You can easily earn thousands of rupees per month as a motion graphic designer. Obviously, it’s not set in stone. Salaries for motion graphic designers vary heavily based on the skills, years of experience, location of the company, and the size of the company.

What Does a Motion Graphic Designer Do?

Motion graphic designers are really creative people who make visually appealing and dynamic graphic content for various media channels as per the requirements of the project. It’s a really cool job, to be honest. They work on animation, visual design, audio, and special effects to infuse life into content. This makes the content more engaging, attractive, and interactive. It is mostly used in videos, advertisements, social media posts, etc.

In modern times, if you have the required skills and palpable creativity, one doesn’t need a degree to go into motion design. Still, a bachelor’s degree in design, computer graphics, animation, media, or art may be required to pursue a high-paying career in motion graphics.

When it comes to the required skills or tools for a motion design professional, they must be adept at using Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, Cinema4D, and other important design tools. Other than these, they must have a good understanding of typography, art and sketching, color theory, and good animation skills to succeed as a motion graphics designer.

Motion Graphics Designer Salary

A career in motion graphics offers a great salary given you have good skills, knowledge of art and aesthetics, and the ability to turn ideas into visual reality. Mastering a few tools and keeping yourself updated with the latest developments would ensure that you land a good salary as a motion graphics designer working a full-time job.

Motion graphic design is a job where you can actually infuse your love for art and creativity and make a career out of it. But let’s be realistic. Everybody needs good money to grow, progress, and sustain their life. So how much does a motion graphic designer make?

Average Salary of Motion Graphics Designer in India

In India, an average motion graphic designer earns around ₹ 4 LPA, with a take-home salary ranging between ₹29,531 – ₹30,852/month based on data of 3,000 salaries (Source: AmbitionBox). On the other hand, the average base pay was found to be ₹50,750 per month, ranging between ₹5,215 – ₹68,800 (Source: Glassdoor).

Overall, after combining various sources and data units, it can be observed that the motion graphic designer’s salary in India varies from Rs 2.5-12 Lakhs per annum. They can expect to take home a monthly salary of ₹25,000-₹85,000 with a variable pay or cash incentive ranging between ₹5,000-₹20,000.

You read the variations in the salary. There’s a range. Why does the range exist? How does the salary in a motion design career vary? Well, as discussed above, the salary will vary as per your experience, skill level, company, location, industry, and so on!

Motion Graphics Designer Salary Based on Location

Motion graphic designer salary varies with location. An area with more opportunities will always present higher pay. So, metropolitans and commercial economic zones are the places you should be targeting to get higher pay and better jobs.

A location having a higher concentration of media houses, marketing, and PR firms or animation studios will allow motion designers to earn more. In fact, product companies constantly demand motion graphic designers to attract more customers for their products.

Following are the motion graphic designer salaries based on location:

Bengaluru: With most of the national and international IT and product companies operational in Bengaluru, it provides a lucrative annual salary package of ₹ 4.5 LPA with an in-hand salary of ₹ 33,547 – ₹ 34,809/month. The salary ranges from ₹2.2-10.7 LPA.

Mumbai: Mumbai is the hub of media and marketing companies. Hence, motion graphic designers receive a competitive salary package of ₹ 4.2 LPA on average with an in-hand salary of ₹ 31,238 – ₹ 32,534/month. The salary ranges from ₹2.1-8.3 LPA.

Delhi: The capital is home to many big companies that offer an attractive average salary of ₹ 4.2 LPA with an in-hand salary of ₹ 31,138 – ₹ 32,435/month to a motion graphic designer. The salary ranges from ₹2-8 LPA.

Gurugram: Gurugram hosts numerous media, marketing, and product companies and has a strategic location. Because of this, it provides an incredible annual salary package of ₹ 4.7 LPA on average with an in-hand salary of ₹ 35,154 – ₹ 36,391/month for a motion graphic designer. The salary ranges from ₹2.1-10 LPA.

Hyderabad: Hyderabad has many companies providing media and PR services and international companies hiring motion designers. It offers a good annual salary of ₹ 4.2 LPA with an in-hand salary of ₹ 31,138 – ₹ 32,435/month on average. The salary ranges from ₹1.9-8.0 LPA.

Chennai: A budding location for the promising IT and product industry, Chennai offers a satisfactory average salary package of ₹ 3.6 LPA with an in-hand salary of ₹ 26,291 – ₹ 27,686/month. The salary ranges from ₹1.9-7.2 LPA.

Note: The data is subject to change as it is based on the received dataset. It could be higher or lower than the mentioned value in some cases. Source: AmbitionBox. Moreover, the salaries are indicative and generic, they can vary further based on multiple other factors.

Motion Graphics Designer Salary Based on Experience

With experience, comes the money. It’s that simple. a fresher will any day get paid less than a motion graphic artist with 3-5 years of experience. Gradually work on your skills, and learn about the applications and business aspects of motion graphics while you work. Indulge in more projects and high-value freelance projects. This will increase your value and experience not only in terms of the number of years but also in terms of your skills and expertise.

Here’s how the motion graphics designer’s salary varies with experience:

0-1 Year/Fresher: As a fresher motion graphic designer, you can be offered an average salary of ₹ 2.4 LPA, extending from ₹ 1.0 LPA to 4.8 LPA. At this stage, you’ll be expected to learn key job skills while working on the initial phase of your career. The average in-hand salary is around ₹ 16,678 – ₹ 18,391/month.

1-3 Years: After gaining some expertise in the area, you’ll be expected to be familiar with the tricks of the trade. At this stage, you can expect an average salary of ₹ 3.0-3.8 LPA, while ranges between ₹ 2-7 LPA. The average take-home salary is around ₹ 21,498 – ₹ 29,675/month.

3-5 Years: After 3 years of work experience, you’ll transition into an experienced motion graphic designer who is an asset to the organization. At this stage, you can expect an average salary of ₹ 4.5-5.0 LPA, while ranges between ₹ 3-9 LPA. The average take-home salary is around ₹ 33,547 – ₹ 38,765/month.

5-7 Years: Working for 5+ years in the motion graphics industry will make you a noticeable and respected voice in the room. With your experience, you’ll be able to lead the motion graphics department of your organization. At this stage, you can expect an average salary of ₹ 5.6-6.0 LPA, while ranges between ₹ 4.2-10 LPA. The average take-home salary is around ₹ 42,384 – ₹ 46,678/month.

7 years and above: Now you will be expected to be an ultra-experienced industry leader in the motion graphics field. At this stage, you can expect an average salary of ₹ 6.5-8.0 LPA, while ranges between ₹ 7.5-11.5 LPA. The average take-home salary is around ₹ 55,730 – ₹ 66,597/month.

Note: The data is subject to change as it is based on the received dataset. It could be higher or lower than the mentioned value in some cases. Source: AmbitionBox. Moreover, the salaries are indicative and generic, they can vary further based on multiple other factors.

Motion Graphics Designer Salary Based on Company Type or Organization

Do you want to work for a big company or looking to get your big start with a startup? The salary of a motion graphic designer will also vary with the scale and type of the organization.

A big company might pay more as the scale and stakes of the work could be large. Yet, mass recruiter companies might not always provide a competitive package. On the other hand, a startup might hire fewer motion designers but might provide a lucrative package for a variety of work and developments.

Here’s how motion graphic designer salary varies based on the company type or organization:

Startup: Startups may or may not provide a big salary, but they look for top-tier professionals. Since they don’t hire in bulk usually, they can provide competitive salaries to deserving candidates. The average salary is ₹ 5.5 LPA, ranging from ₹ 2.2-11.8 LPA. This way, the average in-hand salary of a motion graphic designer falls around ₹ 41,848 – ₹ 42,986/month.

Public: The public sector is known for its initiative for the public good. As a result, a lot of public sector companies are investing in good motion graphic designers to deliver competent products to the common consumers. The Average salary of a motion graphic designer in the Public sector is ₹ 4.8 LPA, ranging from ₹ 2.4-9.0 LPA. This translates to an average in-hand salary of ₹ 35,957 – ₹ 37,183/month,

Fortune 500: Fortune 500 companies comprise the big league of product and service industries. These big, well-reputed companies offer a great average salary of ₹ 5.2 LPA to motion graphic designers which ranges from ₹ 1.1-9.3 LPA. Given that, the average in-hand salary comes around ₹ 39,170 – ₹ 40,348/month.

Global 2000: The Global 2000 include the top companies that hire motion graphic designers to scale their product and services and expand their audience. They pay an average salary of ₹ 5.4 LPA, ranging from ₹ 2.7-11.4 LPA. This brings an average in-hand salary of ₹ 40,777 – ₹ 41,931/month.

Conglomerate: Conglomerates are the companies that rule the market. They are highly reputed and coveted but most of them hire in bulk for a variety of products and services across industries. Hence, they offer a good average salary of ₹ 4.7 LPA starting from ₹ 3.0 and going up to ₹ 10.5 LPA usually. The average in-hand salary of a motion graphic designer in a conglomerate company is estimated to be ₹ 35,275 – ₹ 36,510/month.

Note: The data is subject to change as it is based on the received dataset. It could be higher or lower than the mentioned value in some cases. Source: AmbitionBox. Moreover, the salaries are indicative and generic, they can vary further based on multiple other factors.

Motion Graphics Designer Salary Based on Industry

As a person interested in motion graphic design, you must have noticed that the media, entertainment, gaming, marketing, and advertising industries value your creativity the most. So who’s more likely to provide a higher salary? Naturally, these domains.

Other industries might also pose a demand for talented motion designers, based on their products and services. For example, a brand for kids might use more animation, special effects, and motion graphics to entertain and attract their target audience.

Here’s how the motion graphic designer salary varies based on the industry:

Media, Events, and Entertainment: The media, events, and entertainment industries are some of the biggest appointers of motion graphic designers because they constantly need engaging motion graphics to present information in a better manner. The industry offers an average salary of ₹ 4.8 LPA, ranging between ₹ 2.4-12.0 LPA. A motion graphic designer working in the media industry gets an average in-hand salary of ₹ 35,957 – ₹ 37,183/month.

Marketing, PR, and Advertising: Marketing, PR, and advertising are other industries that constantly require high-quality motion graphics to scale their products. Hence they offer a suitable average salary of ₹ 4.9 LPA ranging between ₹ 2.6-8.5 LPA, with an average in-hand salary of around ₹ 36,359 – ₹ 37,578/month.

Animation and VFX: Motion graphics are often used interchangeably with animation. Hence, the animation and VFX industries are constantly looking for talented motion graphic designers. For that, they pay an average salary of ₹ 3.4 LPA between the range of ₹ 2.1-6.0 LPA. The average in-hand salary comes around ₹ 24,310 – ₹ 25,734/month.

Consulting: Motion graphics help the consulting industry to get the concept across more effectively. The average salary for a motion graphics designer working in the consulting industry is a good ₹ 5.5 LPA, which runs between the range of ₹ 1.0-7.0 LPA. The average in-hand salary is around ₹ 41,580 – ₹ 42,722/month.

Design: Various design fields like interior design, graphic design, industrial design, etc. extensively employ motion graphics design in their day-to-day workings. The average motion graphics designer’s salary in the design industry is ₹ 4.2 LPA. The average in-hand salary is ₹ 31,138 – ₹ 32,435/month, ranging from ₹ 2.4-7.0 LPA.

IT and Software Product: IT and software product companies heavily rely on motion graphics to properly demonstrate their products and services and create awareness about them. They offer a competitive average salary is ₹ 5.1 LPA between the ranges of ₹ 2.7-12.0 LPA for good motion graphic designers. The average in-hand salary comes around ₹ 38,046 – ₹ 39,240/month.

Retail: Retail is another booming industry that offers competitive packages to motion graphic designers. The average salary of motion graphic designers in the retail industry is ₹ 4.5 LPA, ranging between ₹ 2.7-10.0 LPA. At the same time, the average in-hand salary comes to around ₹ 33,410 – ₹ 34,673/month.

Edtech: After the pandemic, Edtech became the next big thing. Educators and courses use a lot of quality motion graphics to develop their modules and lesson videos. As a result, edtech companies hire a lot of motion graphic designers at an average salary of ₹ 4.6 LPA, ranging from ₹ 2.2-7.5 LPA. The average in-hand salary comes around ₹ 33,949 – ₹ 35,204/month.

Fintech: As the young earning force is expanding, so is their need to track, invest, and utilize money efficiently. In the digital age of digital banking, transactions, and currency, fintech is another industry that’s touching the sky. As a result, fintech is using motion graphic design highly and actively hiring professionals for a whopping average salary is ₹ 7.3 LPA, ranging from ₹ 3.6-17.9 LPA. The average in-hand salary is between ₹ 54,579 – ₹ 55,943/month.

Content Development: Content development and content creation are one of the most trending industries in modern times. Content creation uses motion graphics extensively to make the content more engaging and attractive. The average salary of a motion graphic designer working in the content development industry is ₹ 5.2 LPA, ranging from ₹ 2.4-11.9 LPA. The average in-hand salary thus falls somewhere around ₹ 39,170 – ₹ 40,348/month.

Note: The data is subject to change as it is based on the received dataset. It could be higher or lower than the mentioned value in some cases. Source: AmbitionBox. Moreover, the salaries are indicative and generic, they can vary further based on multiple other factors.

To Conclude

Now you know, motion graphic designer salary in India will come under the satisfactory to good category. You can work on upskilling and keeping up with the latest developments in the motion graphics field. Doing quality projects and online certificate courses will further help you get a better salary.

Look for jobs at places that have good infrastructure for corporate development. If you have a penchant for art, aesthetics, and storytelling through visual media, motion graphics is the best field for you to build a successful career. Rest assured, the salary will keep on increasing with your experience, so just stay consistent and let your craft speak.
