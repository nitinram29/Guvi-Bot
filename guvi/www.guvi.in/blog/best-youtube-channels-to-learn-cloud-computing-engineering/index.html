Now, how do you learn about something as big and important as cloud computing? Well, that’s where YouTube comes into play. YouTube isn’t just a place for funny videos. It’s a goldmine of information and learning. There are some fantastic YouTube channels that teach you about cloud computing. They use videos to explain complex ideas in a simple way.

In this blog, we’re going to take you on a tour of the best YouTube channels where you can learn all about cloud computing. We’ll look at what makes each channel special, what topics they cover, and how they can help you understand this exciting world. By the time you finish reading, you’ll be all set to start your own adventure in learning about cloud computing.

Top YouTube Channels for Cloud Computing Engineering

Here’s an organized list of some of the top YouTube channels for learning Cloud Computing Engineering, including a brief description, key features, notable playlists or videos, presenter’s expertise, and subscriber count and engagement:

1. A Cloud Guru

The “Cloud Guru” YouTube channel is a great place for people who want to learn about cloud computing. Whether you’re just starting or you’re already a cloud expert, this channel is friendly and easy to follow. They talk about many different things related to the cloud. The “Cloud Guru” YouTube channel began in January 2015 and has about 140,000 people who subscribe to it. They’ve got over 4,941,083 views on their videos.

They explain these topics in a way that’s easy to understand. So, if you want to learn the basics, get ready for cloud certifications, or just stay updated on cloud news, this channel is for you. The Cloud Guru team is nice, and they know a lot about the cloud. They make learning about the cloud fun and easy.

Kickstart your career by enrolling in GUVI’s Cloud Computing Career Program with Microsoft Azure where you will master technologies like Matplotlib, Pandas, SQL, NLP, Deep Learning,  and build interesting real-life cloud computing projects.

2. AWS Training and Certification

This YouTube channel is a great place to learn about cloud computing. They make short, easy-to-understand videos based on their own experience. You’ll find their videos useful because they keep things simple and practical. Whether you’re new to cloud computing or want to learn more, this channel is a good choice. They started making videos in 2017 and have 82.6K people following them. Their videos have been watched over 4,007,178 times!

Their videos are like quick lessons that give you the essential information without making things too complicated. So, if you’re looking for a straightforward way to understand cloud computing, this channel is a helpful resource.

3. Google Cloud Platform

The official YouTube channel for Google Cloud’s technical community is like a helpful friend for learning about computers. They have easy-to-follow videos showing you how things work, exciting demonstrations, and news about their latest tools.

The official YouTube channel for Google Cloud’s technical community has been a hub for tech enthusiasts since its launch on July 22, 2014. With a staggering 1.06 million subscribers and an impressive 79,643,128 views, this channel has become a go-to resource for individuals eager to learn about technology.

It’s a great place to keep up with what’s happening in the world of technology. Whether you’re a pro or just getting started, this channel has something useful for you. It’s not only about keeping you in the know about cool technology but also showing you tools that can help you make new things.

With clear and easy-to-understand videos, it’s a great place to learn about Google Cloud. You can subscribe to stay updated and make the most of Google Cloud for your projects.

4. Microsoft Azure

The official Microsoft Azure YouTube channel is a must for anyone interested in staying up-to-date with the latest cloud computing products and solutions. The channel features training demos that explain complex concepts, making them accessible to everyone. The Microsoft Azure YouTube channel started in 2008 and has around 299,000 subscribers who’ve watched its videos over 24 million times.

It’s the go-to destination for technical insights that are not only relevant but crucial in the ever-evolving world of cloud computing. What sets Microsoft Azure apart is its commitment to helping you “invent with purpose.”

Whether you’re a seasoned professional or just beginning your cloud computing journey, this channel gives you the knowledge and tools to drive innovation.

Alternatively, if you would like to explore Microsoft Azure through a Self-Paced course, try GUVI’s Azure Self-Paced certification course.

5. Cloud Academy

Cloud Academy is like a smart school for technology. It helps people learn the skills they need in a way that’s just right for them. They teach about big computer systems like Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud. It began on April 22, 2014. So far, it has been seen over 3.2 million times and has about 36.4 thousand people following it.

They also teach important methods like DevOps, FinOps, and security, and cool concepts like AI, machine learning, and data analytics. Cloud Academy is all about helping people everywhere learn the skills they need for jobs in technology. 

Specific Cloud Computing Topics Covered

The range of cloud computing topics covered by these YouTube channels are as follows:

Cloud Service Providers (AWS, Azure, GCP, etc.)

A Cloud Guru: A Cloud Guru provides comprehensive content on cloud service providers such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud Platform. They offer in-depth certification training, tutorials, and exam preparation.

AWS Training and Certification: The AWS official channel delivers authoritative insights on AWS services, exam readiness, and real-world use cases.

Google Cloud Platform: Google’s official channel offers detailed information about Google Cloud services, product deep dives, and customer stories.

Microsoft Azure: The official Microsoft Azure channel provides valuable tutorials, best practices, and in-depth discussions on Azure services.

Cloud Academy: Cloud Academy covers a variety of cloud providers, including AWS, Azure, GCP, Oracle, and Alibaba. They offer training, certifications, and skill assessments.

Virtualization and Containerization

A Cloud Guru: A Cloud Guru explores virtualization, containers, and orchestration platforms like Docker and Kubernetes in detail.

DevOps and CI/CD

A Cloud Guru: This channel provides extensive content on DevOps practices, CI/CD pipelines, and tools.

Cloud Academy: Cloud Academy’s content includes DevOps, FinOps, and CI/CD topics.

Serverless Computing

A Cloud Guru: A Cloud Guru covers serverless computing extensively, focusing on AWS Lambda and Azure Functions.

AWS Training and Certification: The official AWS channel features in-depth content on serverless technologies.

Cloud Security

A Cloud Guru: A Cloud Guru offers cloud security training and guidance.

Data Storage and Databases

A Cloud Guru: This channel offers detailed insights into cloud-based data storage and databases.

Cloud Academy: Cloud Academy covers data storage and databases in the cloud.

Cloud Networking

A Cloud Guru: A Cloud Guru includes cloud networking as part of its comprehensive cloud training.

The depth and quality of content on these channels are generally excellent. They provide tutorials, demonstrations, hands-on labs, and real-world examples to help learners understand and master cloud computing topics. Most of the content is presented by experts and often aligns with certification preparation. Subscribers can find content suitable for all levels, from beginners to advanced users.

Please note that the specific video content may change over time, so it’s a good idea to explore these channels directly for the latest updates on the topics mentioned.

Advantages of Learning Cloud Computing through YouTube

YouTube is an increasingly popular platform for education, offering a host of benefits for learners. Here are some key advantages:

  1. YouTube is a free platform accessible to anyone with an internet connection. It’s available on various devices, from smartphones and tablets to computers and smart TVs, making it a convenient and easily accessible learning resource.
  1. Many educational YouTube channels offer high-quality content for free. This makes it a cost-effective option for self-learners and those seeking to expand their knowledge without incurring significant expenses.
  1. YouTube hosts a vast array of educational content on virtually any topic. Learners can find videos, tutorials, lectures, and demonstrations on subjects ranging from academic topics to practical skills, including cloud computing.
  1. Video content is highly engaging, making it easier for learners to grasp complex concepts. Visual and auditory elements enhance understanding, making it especially effective for visual learners.
  1. YouTube allows learners to control the pace of their education. They can pause, rewind, and replay videos to reinforce their understanding of the material.
  1. Many YouTube channels have active communities in the comment sections. This enables learners to ask questions, share insights, and learn from others with similar interests.

These advantages make YouTube a valuable platform for self-learning, supplementing traditional education, and staying updated in a rapidly changing world. With a diverse range of content and accessible features, YouTube opens the door to a world of knowledge and skill development.

Conclusion

These YouTube channels offer valuable resources for learning cloud computing engineering, whether you’re a beginner or an advanced user. Make sure to explore their content, subscribe, and engage with the community to enhance your cloud computing knowledge and skills.

Now, it’s your turn! Start your cloud computing adventure by subscribing to these channels. Keep learning because the tech world keeps changing. Lifelong learning is how you stay on top of things in the tech world.

So, don’t stop now. Click those subscribe buttons and get started. Your exciting journey in cloud computing begins today, and there’s a whole world of knowledge waiting for you!

FAQs

Are these YouTube channels suitable for beginners, or are they more advanced?

The YouTube channels mentioned in the blog cater to a wide range of learners. While some content may be more advanced, many of these channels offer beginner-friendly materials and tutorials. You can choose content that matches your skill level and gradually progress to more advanced topics as you gain confidence.

Is learning from YouTube as effective as traditional courses or certifications?

Learning from YouTube can be highly effective, especially when complemented with hands-on practice. However, it’s important to note that YouTube channels are typically self-paced and may not provide formal certifications. They are a great way to build practical skills and gain knowledge but may be used alongside formal courses or certifications for a more comprehensive education.

How do I stay updated with the latest cloud computing developments and technology changes?

YouTube channels are a fantastic resource for staying updated with cloud computing trends and changes. To keep up with the latest developments, subscribe to these channels and turn on notifications. Additionally, consider joining online cloud computing communities, following relevant blogs, and attending webinars or industry events to stay informed about the rapidly evolving field of cloud computing.
