What is AWS Certification?

AWS Certification refers to the professional credentials offered by Amazon Web Services (AWS), one of the leading cloud computing platforms. AWS certifications validate the knowledge and skills of individuals in various roles related to AWS cloud services. These certifications are recognized globally and demonstrate proficiency in designing, deploying, and managing applications on the AWS platform.

AWS certifications require passing a corresponding exam, which typically consists of multiple-choice questions and hands-on scenarios. They demonstrate your knowledge and can enhance your career prospects in cloud computing, as AWS is widely used by organizations worldwide. We’ll be talking about some of the best AWS Certifications later in this blog. Keep Reading!

Are AWS Certifications Worth in 2024?

Yes, AWS certifications continue to hold value and are worth pursuing in 2024. While the value of certifications can vary based on individual circumstances and career goals, AWS certifications continue to be highly regarded in the industry and can provide numerous benefits for professionals in 2024 and beyond.

Why Should I Take AWS Certification – Benefits of AWS Certifications

There are several benefits to earning AWS certifications. Here are some of the key benefits it has:

  1. Industry Recognition: AWS certifications are globally recognized and respected in the IT industry. They validate your expertise and proficiency in AWS services and demonstrate your commitment to continuous learning and professional development.
  2. Enhanced Career Opportunities: AWS certifications can significantly improve your career prospects. Many employers look for AWS-certified professionals when hiring for cloud-related roles. Holding certification can make your resume stand out and increase your chances of landing job interviews and promotions.
  3. Expanded Knowledge and Skills: The process of preparing for AWS certifications involves studying and gaining in-depth knowledge of AWS services, architectures, best practices, and industry standards. This knowledge enhances your technical skills and equips you to design, deploy, and manage applications and infrastructure on the AWS platform.
  4. Confidence and Credibility: Earning an AWS certification can boost your confidence in working with AWS services. It provides a sense of validation and credibility, both for yourself and for potential employers or clients. This confidence can positively impact your performance and decision-making when working on AWS projects.
  5. Access to Exclusive Resources: AWS offers various resources and benefits to certified professionals. This includes access to exclusive communities, forums, and events where you can network with other certified individuals and AWS experts. You may also gain access to additional training materials, whitepapers, and technical support.
  6. Stay Up-to-Date with AWS: AWS certifications require periodic recertification to ensure that certified professionals stay updated with the latest advancements and changes in AWS services. This helps you stay current with evolving technologies, trends, and best practices in the cloud computing industry.
  7. Potential Salary Increase: AWS certifications are often associated with higher earning potential. According to industry reports, certified AWS professionals tend to earn higher salaries compared to their non-certified counterparts. This can vary based on factors such as location, experience, and job role, but the certifications can contribute to salary negotiations and career advancement.

Overall, AWS certifications provide a tangible way to showcase your skills, increase your marketability, and open up new opportunities in the dynamic field of cloud computing.

Best and Highest-Paying AWS Certifications

There are several different AWS certifications available, catering to different job roles and levels of expertise. Some of the popular AWS certifications include:

  1. AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner: This entry-level certification validates foundational knowledge of AWS services, cloud concepts, security, pricing, and architectural best practices.
  2. AWS Certified Developer – Associate: This certification focuses on developing and maintaining applications on the AWS platform, covering topics such as AWS services, serverless technologies, storage, and security.
  3. AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate: This certification is designed for individuals who design and deploy scalable and fault-tolerant systems on AWS. It covers architectural best practices, AWS services, and cost optimization.
  4. AWS Certified SysOps Administrator – Associate: This certification validates technical expertise in deploying, managing, and operating systems on AWS. It covers topics such as deployment, monitoring, scalability, and troubleshooting.
  5. AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Professional: This advanced-level certification is for experienced solution architects who design and deploy complex applications on AWS. It focuses on architectural design, migration strategies, and advanced AWS services.
  6. AWS Certified DevOps Engineer – Professional: This certification validates expertise in implementing and managing DevOps practices on the AWS platform. It covers topics such as continuous delivery, automation, and monitoring.

FAQs on AWS Certifications

Q1. Which cloud certification is best in 2024?

Ans. Some of the best cloud certifications in 2024 are –

1. AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Professional
2. Microsoft Certified: Azure Solutions Architect – Expert
3. Salesforce Certified Technical Architect
4. Azure Fundamentals by Microsoft
5. AWS Certified DevOps Engineer – Professional

Q2. Is AWS in demand in 2024?

Ans. AWS is and will be in demand for the upcoming years and major companies are shifting towards cloud services and cloud-based systems. It is the most important and high-demanding skill you should have. AWS owns around 33% of the cloud market.

Q3. Is being AWS-certified worth it?

Ans. Being AWS certified boosts the rate of salary you can ask for in companies. According to research, 73% of AWS professionals stated that they faced an increase in salary after pursuing the certification.

Q4. What is the salary after AWS certification?

Ans. The average salary you can get after being AWS certified ranges from 3 LPA to 28 LPA in India depending upon the skills, expertise, and experience. In the United States, the entry-level salary is $138000.

Q5. Does AWS need coding?

Ans. No, you need not be a pro in coding, just basic knowledge is enough if you want to get into AWS. With only basic knowledge, you can easily develop and deploy apps to the AWS cloud.
